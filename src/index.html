Whats a Galactic Age Calculator?

Put simply, it calculates what your age would be based on a solar year on different planets. We consider a year to be one rotation around the sun so our solar year is 365 days; however Mercury for example takes only 88 days to rotate around the sun making its solar year 88 days long.

Type in your age below and hit calculate to see what your age would be on other planets!
