Whats a Galactic Age Calculator?

Put simply, a galactic age calculator calculates what your age would be based on the solar year of different planets. We consider a year to be one rotation around the sun so our solar year is 365 days; however Mercury for example takes only 88 days to rotate around the sun making its solar year 88 days.
